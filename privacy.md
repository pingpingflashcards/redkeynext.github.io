Welcome to RedKey ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website ([Insert Website URL]) and use our license key management services (the "Service").

### 1. Information We Collect
We collect information that identifies, relates to, describes, or could reasonably be linked to you ("Personal Information").

*   **Account & Purchase Information:** When you purchase a license key or subscribe to a plan, we collect your email address. We do not process or store your raw payment details (e.g., credit card numbers); these are securely handled by our payment processor, Stripe. We store identifiers returned by Stripe (such as your Stripe Customer ID and Subscription ID) to manage your account.
*   **Usage & Technical Data:** To enforce usage limits (e.g., our one-trial-per-6-months policy), we collect your IP address. To protect your privacy, we securely hash this IP address using SHA-256 encryption so it is not stored in plain text.
*   **Customer Support Data:** We use Crisp Chat to provide customer support. If you interact with our chat widget, Crisp may collect technical data, your chat history, and any contact information you provide.
*   **Service Usage Data:** We track the usage of your generated License Keys (e.g., credits consumed, validation requests) to accurately deliver the service you paid for.

### 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected information for the following purposes:
*   To generate, validate, and manage your License Keys and usage credits.
*   To process payments, billing, and subscription renewals via Stripe.
*   To prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized sharing of License Keys.
*   To communicate with you regarding your purchases, account status, and support inquiries.
*   To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

However, no electronic transmission over the internet or information storage technology can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.
